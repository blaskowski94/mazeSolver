Enter a text-based maze in the box below. A maze consists of the following 4 characters: # . A B. No other characters may be present. The pound symbol (#) represents a wall. A period (.) represents an open path. The capital letter A is the starting point and the capital letter B is the end. There should be at least one valid path from A to B. All lines of the maze must be the same length. Click one of the maze buttons below to see an example.
